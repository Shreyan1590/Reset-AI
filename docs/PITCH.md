## The Problem (30 seconds)

**Context switching is destroying productivity.**

- Average knowledge worker switches tasks every **3 minutes**
- Takes **23 minutes** to regain full focus after interruption
- **2.1 hours** per day lost to context switching
- Results in mental fatigue, errors, and burnout

**The hidden cost:** Lost time trying to remember "what was I doing?"

---

## The Solution: RESET AI

An intelligent system that:
1. **Detects** when you lose context (tab switches, idle time, confusion)
2. **Captures** your work state automatically (pages, documents, code)
3. **Generates** AI summaries of what you were doing
4. **Restores** your thinking state with one click

**Think of it as "Undo" for your brain.**
